Once upon a time, in a galaxy _not so far away_, there lived a rubber duck named Quirk. Now, this was no ordinary rubber duck; Quirk was destined for greatness, or so he thought.

## Edited in Obsidian

Tired of the **mundane life of floating** in bathtubs and paddling pools, Quirk dreamed of something more... something out of this world. One fateful day, while basking in the warmth of a sunlit bathroom, Quirk found himself suddenly lifted off his watery domain by an unexpected gust of wind. Spiraling through the air, Quirk felt a surge of excitement as he soared higher and higher, propelled by forces unknown.
